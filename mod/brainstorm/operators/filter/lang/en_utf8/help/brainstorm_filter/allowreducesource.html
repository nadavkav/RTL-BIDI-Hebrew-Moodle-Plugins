Parameter: Allow reduce source

When enabled, the participants are allowed to strip off the eliminated inputs from the source input set. This may be usefull in a decision process where there are many contributors, or when a large amount of inputs were given.

Beware that this will delete all related data for the deleted entries and for all operators. This may perturbate the results of other operators.
