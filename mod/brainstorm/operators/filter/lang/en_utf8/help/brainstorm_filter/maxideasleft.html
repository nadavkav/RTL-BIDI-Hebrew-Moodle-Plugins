Parameter: Maximum number of ideas left

This parameter allows forcing participants to keep a certain amount of ideas from the source, thus making mandatory to select sufficiant ideas to eliminate to comply with the constraint.

If selecting ideas to delete seems being a problem for participants, the "pair comparison procedure" might be tried to arbitrate the filtering decision.
