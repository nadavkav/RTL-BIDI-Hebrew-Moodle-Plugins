Phase : Display all results

This activity phase allow viewing all results, compared to its own result set.

Each operator will provide visualisation models that fits with, depending on the organisation that has been set up. You might discover what xere the choices of other participants and argue on that choices.

You may also view some properties of some organisation, e.g. check cycles in relationships when mapping ideas together, this might be valuable in some experiences.

If the Brainstormer is set to parallel flow mode, you may freely revert to previous phases you were given access to and change data. In sequential mode, you will not allowed to change data any more when the module is set to its display situation.
