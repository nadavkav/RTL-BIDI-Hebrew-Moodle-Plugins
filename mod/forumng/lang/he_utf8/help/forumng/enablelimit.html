Limit user posting

In some specialised forum use it might be helpful to limit the number of posts by an individual user. This feature provides that option.

You can choose the number of posts a user is limited to, and the time period (day, 2 days, week, or fortnight). The time period is always considered from current rather than as discrete units; for example, if you are limited to 10 posts per one day and you make 10 posts on 23:00 one day, you cannot make another post until 23:00 the next day.

When users have 3 or fewer 'remaining' posts and go to make a new one, a warning displays. When they have no remaining posts, an error appears instead. The error tells them the date and time at which they will next be allowed to post.

Users with the mod/forumng:ignorethrottling capability are not affected by post limits.
